About Fibonacci Clock

The Fibonacci Clock represents time using squares sized according to the Fibonacci sequence (1, 1, 2, 3, 5).

How to Read

  • Red squares represent hours
  • Green squares represent minutes
  • Blue squares represent both
  • White squares are inactive
